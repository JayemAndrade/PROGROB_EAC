About Hyrdoblazed X

Before creating Hydroblazed X, we first developed a remote control system for our performance task. We explored the mechanics and functionality of a remote-controlled robot, ensuring smooth communication between the controller and the device. This activity enhanced our understanding of automation, wireless technology, and real-world applications of robotics.

Example Video

Building on this foundation, we then designed Hydroblazed X, a firetruck robot equipped with sensors to detect fires. This project allowed us to apply our knowledge of robotics and automation to create a functional and responsive firefighting system. If you want to see updates on what we're working on, click this Latest Post and support Hydroblazed X!

Materials For Remote Controller

  • Transmitter (Controller)
  • Arduino (Uno/Nano)
  • nRF24L01 module
  • Joystick module (for movement control)
  • Buttons (for siren and water pump)
  • LED indicators (optional)
  • Power supply (Battery or USB)

    • Materials For HydroBlazed X

  • Arduino (Uno/Nano)
  • nRF24L01 module
  • Motor Driver (L298N or DRV8833)
  • DC Motors (for movement)
  • Mini Water Pump (controlled via relay or MOSFET)
  • Servo Motor (for water nozzle movement)
  • Buzzer (for siren)
  • LEDs (for emergency lights)
  • Power supply (Li-ion battery pack)
